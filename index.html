About Me

I am a passionate and expressive visual artist rooted in the rich traditions of Indian art. With a strong foundation in painting, sketching, and visual storytelling, I draw inspiration from nature, emotions, mythology, and the rhythms of everyday life. I am a seeker of color, texture, and symbolism—blending them to craft soulful narratives that transcend the canvas. My work is a reflection of devotion, spirit, and identity, inviting viewers into a world where culture and creativity converge. I am constantly evolving—exploring new techniques, mediums, and perspectives. Through exhibitions, digital platforms, and collaborative journeys, I continue to share my artistic voice and connect with those who find meaning in every stroke and story..
